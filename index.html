Biography

I am Assistant Professor of Statistics at Purdue University. Prior to joining purdue in 2021, I completed my Ph.D. in Statistics at Harvard University, under the supervision of Pierre Jacob. My research interests include agent-based models and Monte Carlo methods.

My Chinese name is written as 鞠念桥 and you can also call me Phyllis. You can contact me at `nianqiao AT purdue DOT edu'.

I maintain a blog at phylliswithdata.com.
